The marketing landscape is filled with buzzwords. Two of the most common are **Performance Marketing** and **Growth Marketing**. While they share similarities, they require different mindsets.

### Performance Marketing
Is highly focused on short-term results and specific actions (like a purchase or a lead). It's all about **ROI** and immediate profitability.

### Growth Marketing
Growth marketing is more holistic. It looks at the **entire funnel** (from awareness to retention). It's not just about getting the customer to buy once, but keeping them.

### Which one do you need?
Both. You need performance techniques to acquire customers efficiently, but you need a growth strategy to build long-term value.
